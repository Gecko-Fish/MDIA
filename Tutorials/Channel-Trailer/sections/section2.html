The first step after you load into the landing page you need to create your HeyGen account. You can use Google/Meta/Customized account to sign in. You can set you account or log out through the icon in the red cricle.

Access Runway

Click on the button “TRY RUNWAY FOR FREE >” to create an account with Runway or login if you already have an account created.
