Step 2: Prompting the Runway tool

In this workflow, we will be using Runway’s text-to-image generative AI tool. To access this tool, click on the “Start with Text” button.

Here you will see a text box. Type in whatever prompt you want. A prompt is the video you would like to create and what you would like the video to contain.  For example, you can type

Cartoon girl looking out to the sunset in Kyoto

You can also type the following to specify what kind of movement you would like the characters or the scene in your video to do.

Cartoon girl looking out to the sunset in Kyoto, gentle movement
