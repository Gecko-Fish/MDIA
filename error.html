Looks like Javascript is not enabled

Google Chrome:

  1. Click on the three dots in the top-right corner and select "Settings".
  2. Scroll down and click on "Advanced".
  3. Under the "Privacy and security" section, click on "Site Settings".
  4. Scroll down and click on "JavaScript".
  5. Toggle the switch to enable JavaScript.

Mozilla Firefox:

  1. Click on the menu button (three horizontal lines) and select "Preferences".
  2. Go to the "Privacy & Security" panel.
  3. Scroll down to the "Permissions" section.
  4. Check the box next to "Enable JavaScript".

Microsoft Edge:

  1. Click on the menu button (three dots) and select "Settings".
  2. Scroll down and click on "View advanced settings".
  3. Under the "Privacy and services" section, toggle the switch to enable JavaScript.

Safari:

  1. Open Safari and click on "Safari" in the menu bar.
  2. Select "Preferences".
  3. Go to the "Security" tab.
  4. Check the box next to "Enable JavaScript".
