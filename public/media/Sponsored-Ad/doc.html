Editing an Ad Sponsored Video Workflow

Video content creators need to create content for brand partnerships, which typically involves a video advertisement of a brand’s product. By following this workflow, you’ll learn how to utilize generative AI tools to speed up the process of creating your sponsored ad video for a long-form content platform like YouTube and a short-form content platform like TikTok or Instagram Reels.

Difficulty: Easy

Tools:

  • Your own footage of the product
  • CapCut Ad Script
  • ChatGPT
  • CapCut
  • CapCut Upscaling

Overview:

Capcut ad script (for the idea) -> ChatGPT to edit the script’s tone, length, etc. -> Capcut to add media (media that the user already has

Capcut Ad Script

⚙️Step 1: Head over to capcut.com, and click on the button “<Sign up for free>” to register if you don’t have an account.

⚙️Step 2: If you do not have an account with Capcut, you will be directed to the account creation page you see above. To create an account, please type in your email in the “Enter email” field and press the  “<Continue>” button. You can also log in with your existing Google, Tiktok, Facebook, or CapCut Mobile account, which will take you into that respective account’s login page.

⚙️Step 3: You will be asked to set a password and once you’ve finished, click the “<Sign up>” button. You can also log into your existing account by clicking on the “Sign in” text right below the “<Sign up>” button.


⚙️Step 3. Once you are logged in, click on “Magic Tools” on the sidebar.

⚙️ Step 5: Click on the “Ad script” icon to access the tool.

⚙️ Step 6: You will prompted with 2 questions that asks you to further describe your ad. Enter the name of your product and brand, as well as a few selling points of the product (Max. 150 characters)

⚙️ Step 7: CapCut will generate 5 different script options for you. Choose your favourite script and click on the “copy” icon to copy the text. We’ll use the next tool, ChatGPT, to refine this further.

The script we chose (script 5):

In my daily routine, I make sure to pack my bag with everything I need for the day. Lately, I've been carrying this sunscreen with me everywhere I go. It has an SPF fifty rating and provides both UVA and UVB protection. Its lightweight, fast-drying formula is non-comedogenic and waterproof, leaving no white cast. I trust this sunscreen to protect my skin from harm and keep it looking its best.

ChatGPT (for editing the script)

Note: This workflow goes through how to generate a video script from a video idea that you have using ChatGPT, assuming no prior knowledge of the tool.

⚙️Step 1: To access ChatGPT, please click on this link. If you already have an account created, click on the button “<LOG IN>” or create an account by clicking on “<SIGN UP>” .

⚙️Step 2: If you do not have an account with OpenAI, you will be directed to the account creation page you see above. To create an account, please type in your email in the “Email Address” section and press the  “<Continue>” button. You can also log in with your existing Google, Microsoft, or Apple account, which will take you into that respective account’s login page. You can log into your existing account by clicking on the “Login” text right below the “<Continue>” button.

⚙️Step 3: You will be asked to set a password, please make sure it contains at least 12 characters. Once you’ve finished, click the “<Continue>” button.

⚙️Step 4: You will be redirected to a screen which asks you to verify your email. Please navigate to your respective email inbox. If you don’t receive an email, please click the “<Resend email>” button.

⚙️Step 5: Open your inbox and find the email with a subject line “OpenAI - Verify your email” and click into it. Click on the “<Verify email address>” button.

⚙️Step 6: If you see this page, don’t panic! Navigate to the page you were on before logging into your inbox and refresh the screen.

⚙️Step 7: Next, you’ll see this screen that asks for your full name and birthday. If you’re not comfortable providing this information, you could put in an alias.

⚙️Step 8: A disclaimer will pop up with some tips to get started. Once you’ve read it, click on the “<Okay, let’s go>” button.

⚙️Step 9: Begin typing your prompt into the “Message ChatGPT” box.

This is the prompt we used. Feel free to modify it.

I have this script for a product ad video, help me make it sound more natural, but keep the same format and length: [Paste in script from CapCut]

This is the script ChatGPT generated for us:

Introducing our latest essential: SPF 50 sunscreen. Defend your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays with our lightweight, fast-drying formula. Non-comedogenic and waterproof, it leaves no white cast, ensuring your skin looks its best. Embrace the outdoors with confidence, protected by our trusted sunscreen.

⚙️Step 10: Next, prompt ChatGPT to separate the text to remain consistent with the 3 sections we received from the CapCut Ad Script tool using this prompt:

Can you separate that text into 3 sections: Hook,  Selling Points, Call to action

CapCut

⚙️Step 11: Paste the 3 bodies of text that ChatGPT generated for us into the respective categories on CapCut (Hook, Selling Points, Call to Action),

Then click “Use this Script”, making sure that your script is selected. You can tell it is selected if the script is outlined in teal.

⚙️Step 12: Now, it’s time to add in your own footage that you want to include in your video. Add the videos you would like in its respective part by clicking on the + buttons.

⚙️Step 13: You can drag your footage into the dotted rectangle, or select your video in the browser. You can add as many video clips as you would like.

Repeat this step for the rest of the video footage you have that you want to add to the video.

Once uploaded, select the video(s) you would like to add for the specific section, and the selected videos will show below. Then click on Continue.

⚙️Step 14: Repeat Steps 12-13 for all 3 of the sections

Step 15: For each section, trim the video(s) to the recommended time that CapCut recommends. In our case, it is 4 seconds.

Step 16: You can change the aspect ratio of the video, by clicking on the TikTok preview dropdown, and selecting Original.

Click on the pencil tool to trim the video

You can drag the slider to trim the video. Once it is trimmed to the recommended time, click on done.

Repeat this for all 3 sections

Once you’re happy with the aspect ratio, click “Open multi-track editor” to continue editing

Step 16

You can now preview your final video! Feel free to play around with the video editor to tailor the video to your needs. Once it is done, click on Export to save the video. Click on download to save!

CapCut Upscaler

In order to effectively upscale the quality of your video, you can use the CapCut upscaler.

⚙️ Step Go back to the “Magic Tools” page and scroll down to the “For videos” section, and select “Video upscaler”.

⚙️Step 6. Your video will be previewed here, and you can select what resolution you want to scale up to on the top left selector.

⚙️ Step 7. The video will generate, be patient as this will take awhile. You will know it is done, when it no longer shows “Generating…”, and the export button turns blue.

⚙️ Step 8. Once the video is done generating, click on the export button to save the video.

⚙️Step 9. Select the option to download, and you’re all set!
