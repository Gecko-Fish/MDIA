Filler scenes / b roll footage Workflow

This document outlines a structured workflow for creating a short film using a series of digital tools, including ChatGPT for scriptwriting, Dall-E for scene imagery, Runway for animation, Suno for background music generation, and Premiere or Capcut for video editing. The steps are designed to facilitate the creation of a short film from conception to final edit.

Tools:

1.         ChatGPT: For ideating scripts and storyline development.

2.         Dall-E: For generating detailed images for each scene.

3.         Runway: For animating images into video.

4.         Suno: For generating background music.

5.         Premiere or Capcut: For video editing.

6.   Storyboarder: Advance method for storyboard creating

⚙️Step 1: Utilize ChatGPT to generate a storyline. Here I am using ChatGPT 4.0, but to generate a storyline ChatGPT3.5 would be fine. Type in “write a short script for a cute cat story, provide more detail description of the scenes and generate image of each scene, a long with image prompt”

The following is the detailed description of each scene and the image prompt:

Scene 1: Luna Peers Out

  • Description: It's early morning, and the sun is just beginning to paint the sky with hues of pink and orange. Inside a cozy house, Luna, a small fluffy white cat with emerald green eyes, sits perched on a windowsill, her gaze fixed on the world outside. She's filled with a sense of wonder and an itch for adventure.
  • Image Prompt: "A small, fluffy white cat with emerald green eyes sitting on a windowsill inside a cozy house. Early morning light, with hues of pink and orange, shines through the window, highlighting the cat's curiosity and wonder. The room is warmly lit, suggesting a comfortable, inviting space."

Scene 2: The Great Outdoors

  • Description: Luna finds an open window and leaps through it, landing softly on the lush green grass outside. The garden is a riot of color, with flowers of every hue blooming under the bright sun. Luna looks around, her senses tingling with the sights, sounds, and smells of nature.
  • Image Prompt: "A lush garden scene with a fluffy white cat standing in the center, having just leaped out of an open window. The garden is filled with colorful flowers under a bright sun. The cat looks around curiously, taking in the sights and smells of nature. The scene is vibrant and full of life, reflecting the cat's sense of adventure."

Scene 3: New Friends

  • Description: As Luna ventures further, she encounters a variety of garden inhabitants. A friendly butterfly flutters by, landing gently on her nose, making her sneeze. Next, she meets a cautious but curious squirrel, and they share a moment of silent communication before the squirrel dashes up a tree, with Luna watching in amusement.
  • Image Prompt: "A fluffy white cat in a lush garden, with a butterfly perched on its nose, causing the cat to look cross-eyed with a slight sneeze. Nearby, a squirrel stands on its hind legs, observing the cat with curiosity. The garden is vibrant, showcasing the interaction between the cat and the local wildlife."

Scene 4: The Challenge

  • Description: Luna's attention is caught by a tall tree with branches stretching out like arms ready to embrace the sky. Her adventurous spirit ignited, she decides to climb. Step by step, she makes her way up, determined and focused.
  • Image Prompt: "A fluffy white cat beginning to climb a tall tree with wide, embracing branches under a clear blue sky. The cat looks determined and focused, with its claws gripping the bark tightly. The scene captures the essence of challenge and adventure, showcasing the cat's ambitious ascent."

Scene 5: At the Top

  • Description: Reaching the top, Luna gazes out over the rooftops and into the distant horizon. The view is breathtaking. She sits, her fur ruffled by the gentle breeze, feeling a sense of achievement and wonder. She's the queen of her world, even if just for a moment.
  • Image Prompt: "A fluffy white cat sitting at the top of a tall tree, looking out over rooftops and the distant horizon. The scene is bathed in the golden light of late afternoon, with a gentle breeze ruffling the cat's fur. The cat appears triumphant and contemplative, enjoying the breathtaking view from above."

Scene 6: The Return

  • Description: As the sun begins to set, casting a golden glow over everything, Luna decides it's time to head home. She carefully makes her way down the tree and back through the garden, now familiar and filled with the day's memories. She slips back through the window into the cozy house, ready for a well-deserved nap.
  • Image Prompt: "A fluffy white cat returning to a cozy house through an open window, as the sun sets, casting a golden glow over a lush garden. The cat looks satisfied and a bit tired, carrying the aura of a day well spent on an adventure. The scene is warm and welcoming, signaling the end of a journey."

Scene 7: Luna's Restful Slumber

  • Description:After a day filled with exploration and new experiences, Luna finds her favorite spot in the cozy house—a soft, cushioned windowsill adorned with a warm, knitted blanket. The room is calm, with the soft purr of Luna’s contented slumber filling the air. The sun has set, and the room is aglow with the soft light from a nearby lamp, casting a peaceful ambiance. Luna is curled up, her fluffy tail wrapped around her, whiskers twitching ever so slightly as she dreams of her day’s adventures.

  • Image Prompt:"Create a realistic, detailed image of Luna, a small, fluffy white cat with emerald green eyes, curled up asleep on a cushioned windowsill inside a cozy house. The scene is tranquil, with soft lighting from a nearby lamp accentuating the peaceful expression on the sleeping cat's face. The knitted blanket beneath her adds to the warmth of the scene. The room is quiet, evoking a sense of calm and completion after Luna's day of adventure."

⚙️Step 2: Generate images for each scene using Dall-E. This step involves using the detailed image prompts provided by ChatGPT. For users seeking alternative methods, free text-to-image AI tools like Automatic1111 or Leonardo AI can be explored. Since the initial request to ChatGPT includes generating images, additional prompts for Dall-E may not be necessary.

⚙️Advance Option for Step 2:

If you want to create more stable and uniformed visual style storyboard, we will suggest you to use Storyboard (https://storyboarder.ai/)

After you login and go into your dashboard, click “New Project”

If you have a finished storyboard scene script, please select “Script”, if you only have a basic idea about the story, please select “Concept”. Because this is a advance option, so we use Script as sample in this workflow.

According to your story, you can select the genre and storyboard art style in fit. The screenplay file using the original script file as reference: Screenplay Script - The Last Garden

After you click “Add Project”, you can begin to generate a shotlist based on your script. You can edit if you think the generation is not correct.

When you think the shotlist is well formatted, you can click “Storyboard” on the top of the page, and start generating your storyboard.

You can edit the storyboard script and click “Save” then click “Refresh” for regenerating that single image.

Finally, click “Export” on the top for downloading the shot list and storyboard.

This may involve slight alterations to the initial image prompts

⚙️Step 4: Integrate with Runway for animation

Open up Runway from the website. Link is below

Runway: https://runwayml.com/ 

Once its open navigate to image to text tab

Drag the image of 1st scene from the previous step and used the description for image to video generation. Note there is a word limitation for the word prompt. For this generation, I adjust the generation motion to 4 to ensure smooth transitions and prevent overacting movements.

Repeat the following steps for each scene. You may need to regenerate couple of time as the video generated does not match the prompt of theirs is warping of morphing

Once all the scenes are animated save it to your local folder for further editing

⚙️Step 5: Now we will use Capcut to combine animated videos into a longer sequence using Capcut or Premiere. Start by creating a new project and uploading the clips for editing. Arrange the clips on the timeline, adjusting as necessary for optimal flow.

Upload the clips to the side tab then drag them to the arrow point for further editing.

⚙️Step 6: Adding transition between the scene

To enhance the visual continuity between scenes, I added transitions between each scene

By clicking at the transition tab, it would show options for transition effects. Selecting one that complements the story's mood and style

The one that is used in this tutorial is shaken II, then select the frame between the scenes and simply click the blue + button. And repeat the same step for each transition between scenes

Here i set the duration to 0.3s

⚙️Step 7: Background Enhancement

I want to improve the visual appeal of the background. To do that first select the whole track, and navigate to video tab on the right top corner. Scroll down to Canvas

Blur is utilize for the background. Here I adjust the frame size to eliminate unwanted elements and focus the viewer's attention on the main subjects.

⚙️Step 8: Creating Background music

You could find background music in Capcut by clicking on the audio tab on the left hand corner. Select the ones that you would like to add on to the track. But here I want to introduce a music generator tool, Suno.

Suno website link: https://www.suno.ai/ 

Once login in with your account,  you would see the below image. Navigate to create.

Type in the prompt generated from step 1 “story about a curious little cat named Luna venturing out to discover the magic of her neighborhood” Then click the create button.

Choose one of the generated songs and download it to your local file

⚙️Step 9: Adding Background music

Navigate back to Capcut and import the sound track by dragging it towards the arrow

Since this sound track is 1 minute and 21 seconds long, we will have to shorten it.

Here we would use the autobeat feature, where it could automatically match out 2 levels of beat. Beat 1 is the major beats from the imported music, and beat 2 is all the subset beat and major beat. This is for betting transition when combining with the animation and music. Here I will use Beat 1. And drag the transition point on to the beat.Repeat the steps of each transition scene.

⚙️Step 9: Exporting the video

This would be the last step. Click on export on the right hand corner to export the video.

Choose where to export the video and click on Export button.

Boom You have gone through the whole workflow
