Loop video / AI audio generation

This workflow allows user to create AI-generated relaxing ASMR video with white noise background, the workflow integrates AI tools for music composition, audio mixing, visual creation, animation, and video editing.

⚙️ Tools Used:

  • Create White Noise Background: Utilize AI audio tools capable of generating or layering white noise. This adds to the ASMR experience, providing a soothing background that complements the relaxing music.
  • Generate Visual Assets: Use Midjourney to create static images that reflect the video’s relaxed and ambient nature, such as serene landscapes, cozy indoor scenes, or abstract visuals.
  • Edit Video and Audio: Compile and layer your lo-fi tracks and white noise using CapCut or similar editing software, ensuring a harmonious blend. Edit the animated visuals to match the audio, creating a seamless loop for continuous play.
