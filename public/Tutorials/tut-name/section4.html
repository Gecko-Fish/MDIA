Step 2: Prompting the Runway tool

After you have entered your prompt, click on the “Generate” button on the bottom right hand corner of the textbox. The “4s” represents the duration of your generated video (4s = 4 seconds). Your intro/outro video should now be generated!

To save the video, click on the left most icon on the top right-hand corner of the video.  You now have your very own intro/outro video to use in your content!
